# Bahrain Messaging Hub Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Bahrain Messaging Hub ("BMH", "we", "us", or "our") provides private messaging features for users in Bahrain. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and the choices available to you when you use Bahrain Messaging Hub.

By using Bahrain Messaging Hub, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the information needed to create your account, deliver messages, improve reliability, and keep the service secure.

### A. Information you provide directly

We may collect:

- Your phone number for sign-in and verification
- One-time password verification details needed to confirm your number
- Your profile information, such as your display name, profile photo, and status
- The messages, photos, videos, voice notes, and files you choose to send through the app
- Support requests or feedback you send to us

### B. Information collected from your device with permission

If you grant permission, we may collect:

- Contacts stored on your device, including names and phone numbers, to help you find people you know who use Bahrain Messaging Hub
- Camera access to capture photos or videos you choose to send or use as a profile photo
- Photo library or file access to let you attach media or documents
- Microphone access to record voice notes
- Push notification token information so we can send message alerts to your device

You can decline these permissions, but some features may not work as intended.

### C. Information generated when you use the service

We may collect service data such as:

- Account creation time and profile update timestamps
- Message delivery, read, and sending status information
- Conversation participant and attachment metadata
- Device and app information needed to deliver notifications, protect accounts, and diagnose service issues

## 2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

- Create and secure your account
- Verify your phone number and sign you in
- Deliver messages, attachments, voice notes, and notifications
- Sync your contacts so you can identify people you may want to message
- Show profile details and read receipts
- Maintain service security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot problems
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

## 3. End-to-End Encryption and Metadata

BMH is designed with a security-first architecture aligned with national digital trust and data protection principles in the Kingdom of Bahrain

End-to-end encryption does not eliminate all data processing. To operate the service, we still process certain metadata such as phone numbers, profile details, contact matching data, push tokens, conversation membership, message timestamps, delivery and read events, and presence information.BMH cannot access or read message content as encryption keys are stored on user devices.

## 4. When We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information in the following cases:

- With service providers and infrastructure vendors that help us host the app, store encrypted data, and deliver push notifications
- With other users, to the extent your profile information, phone number, presence, and messaging activity are visible as part of the service
- If required by law, regulation, legal process, or valid governmental request
- To protect the rights, safety, security, and integrity of Bahrain Messaging Hub, our users, or the public
- As part of a merger, financing, acquisition, or sale of some or all of our business, subject to appropriate safeguards

## 5. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain security and backup processes.

Retention periods may vary by data type. For example:

- Account and profile information may be retained while your account remains active
- Contact matching data may be retained while needed to support contact discovery and sync
- Message metadata and encrypted message records may be retained to support delivery, history, and account continuity
- Technical logs may be retained for a shorter period for security and reliability purposes

If you want to request deletion of your account or personal information, contact us at `ncst.aird@gmail.com`.

## 6. Your Choices

You can:

- Choose whether to grant access to contacts, camera, microphone, photos, and files
- Edit your profile information inside the app
- Control notification permissions in your device settings
- Request access, correction, or deletion by contacting us

If you remove permissions, some features may become unavailable.

## 7. Security

We use technical and organizational measures intended to protect your information, including encryption features for message content and access controls for service data. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. International Processing

Your information may be processed or stored in countries other than the one where you live, including by our service providers. When this happens, we take reasonable steps intended to protect your information in line with applicable law.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Bahrain Messaging Hub is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us at `ncst.aird@gmail.com` so we can review and address the issue.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and may provide additional notice where required.
